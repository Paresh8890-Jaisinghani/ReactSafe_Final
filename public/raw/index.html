Driver Skills Assessments

  • Reaction Time Test

    Helps drivers respond quickly to what they see or hear.
    Measures reaction time to avoid accidents.
    Tells drivers how well they can think fast.
    Helps improve driving skills and safety.

  • Rapid Quiz

    Tests drivers' knowledge of traffic signals and signs.
    Asks questions quickly to improve awareness.
    Helps drivers understand and remember traffic rules.
    Encourages safer driving and reduces accidents.

  • Obstacle Test

    Simulates real driving situations with obstacles.

    Challenges drivers to navigate and maintain speed.

    Enhances awareness, reflexes, and control.

    Prepares drivers for unexpected road situations.

  • Precision Timer Challenge

    Demands accuracy and speed in timed movements.

    Helps improve control and decision-making skills.

    Fosters quick reflexes and driving proficiency.

    Promotes safer and more confident driving.

  • Traffic Light Simulation

    Tests reaction to changing traffic signals.

    Players wait for green light and press a button.

    Rewards correct responses with points.

    Encourages quick thinking and precision.
